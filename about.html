Our story

A family business, built with love.

Studio Stars since 1974

High quality accommodation, the Zakynthian way.

Welcome to Studio Stars. We are a family business that has been operating successfully for over 20 years in the field of tourism. Our characteristic is the famous Zakynthian hospitality and respect for the customer. We provide an extremely friendly environment, with comfort, clean and stylish rooms, ideal for couples and families looking for a unique experience.

Our goal is to offer you a pleasant and relaxing vacation, which you will remember. Near us you will find mini markets and supermarkets such as AB Vassilopoulos and Lidl. A short distance away are restaurants, cafes and entertainment shops. The bus stop is right outside the accommodation (E.R.A. stop on the Zakynthos – Laganas line). The airport is only 3 km away, as well as great beaches.

Our location is ideal because it shares the distance between the beach of Laganas, Kalamaki and Keri with the main city and port of Zante. There is also a large parking lot for any kind of vehicle, and a playground for our little fellas.
