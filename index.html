Welcome

A small island story, told in three generations.

Studio Stars is a family business that has been operating successfully for over five decades in the heart of Zakynthos. Our trademark is the famous Zakynthian hospitality and a deep respect for every guest who walks through our door.

We provide an extremely friendly environment, with comfort, clean and stylish rooms — ideal for couples and families looking for a unique experience away from the crowd, yet close to everything.

"Our goal is to offer you a pleasant and relaxing vacation that you will remember."
